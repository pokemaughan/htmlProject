Hiking

Equipment: hiking shoes, sunscreen, snacks are a must, water, weather-appropriate clothing, hat and sunglasses are optional

Weather: Ideally, spring to falltime weather. i.e. sunny, maybe light breeze. Don't hike when cold or snowing = much unhappy

The Experience

Hiking helps people gain a greater appreciation for nature. A lot of the trails around provo involve hiking up a mountain and looking at a view. 10 out of 10 would reccommend. These views are breathtaking.

There are other trails that explore interesting land formaitons, such as The Narrows in Zion National Park. Hiking that is mucho bueno also.
