Water Polo

Equipment: water polo swim suit, goal cage, swim cap, water polo cap, sunscreen

Weather: Anything but lightning. Literally, anything.

The Experience Although the name may mislead one to think that it is played with horses, Water Polo is a contact-sport that is like hockey, but in the water. Many take up the sport to keep in shape for competitive swimming, and end up liking water polo better.

Best played when sunny, but doesn't stop one from playing it in the rain, snow, etc. Remember to get out of the water asap when there is a thunder storm.
